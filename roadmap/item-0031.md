Code-refactoring enabler for ADR-047; touches no product principle, only retires unfavorable PoC-era implementation. The runtime currently reconstructs CUE compilation units by string surgery (strip package/import lines, rewrite lane.# to #, concatenate) because cuecontext.CompileString/CompileBytes cannot resolve imports or cross-file references. Replace with native cue/load over the embedded specs module (embed.FS plus load.Config.FS, available since CUE v0.17.0) bundled in one internal/cue package in the foundation layer. Includes bumping cuelang.org/go to v0.17.0 (make generate and make check verified green locally on v0.17.0-rc.1). Unblocks the base-scalars consolidation (item-0024) by making cross-file and cross-package references resolve natively, and subsumes the crossval loader rework (item-0030), which can then be retired.
