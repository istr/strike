Register row R-36, adjudicated in item-0072 as D1 per rulebook RB-06 and ADR-049 (4). DialVerified, its sibling in the same file, already takes the concept; the foreign call is net.Dialer.DialContext one frame deeper. DialTCP is not merged into DialVerified: the two carry opposite IP-literal policies, DialVerified fuses routing and SNI by design, and the mediator needs the raw tls.Conn plus a name-keyed identity that a merge would rekey to the resolved IP.
