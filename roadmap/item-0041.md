Depends on item-0040 (the TLS shape must exist). One of three
TLS-family endpoints that collapse onto the same Address-plus-Trust shape;
among them the protocol does not distinguish, the lane-side role does, which
is why they merge to one concept type. The url:"^https://" identity field is
the representation leak ADR-048 fact 1 forbids; it becomes a boundary
constraint and projection, with the base path carried by Address.path.
Touches the ADR-040 keyless service-client path.
