The collision is most likely a name clash between two concepts rather than true redundancy: the method is the deploy mechanism (kubernetes, registry, custom; a real discriminator) and the target is the deploy destination identity used to pair pre- and post-state digests across deploys, whose type is a free string. The open question is which namespace is authoritative when the method is kubernetes, since both the method (required) and the target (optional) carry one. Resolve by a small reasoning or spike task, not from the cuff. The current lean is rename (disambiguate the target type field) over merge, but the authoritative-namespace question must be settled first. A follow-on fix item applies the ratified decision.
