Lead item of the endpoint-model arc. Ratified design forks (operator):
A2 -- contract/endpoint owns two named endpoint shapes (TLSEndpoint,
SSHEndpoint), not one mega-union; B2 -- the SSH host-key trust stays a
separate shape from Trust because SSH trusts a set of host keys while the
TLS family trusts a single anchor; C -- the endpoint role (egress peer,
service client, resolver) is lane-side, expressed by which lane field holds
the endpoint, not a field on the endpoint type.

This slice also establishes the packed host:port boundary grammar over
Address that items 0041 and 0042 reuse, and removes the host go redirect
footgun on the peer host field by making CUE and Go agree on Address.

Scope boundary: this is the declared axis (Address plus Trust) only. The
observed TLS server identity (EngineServerTLS and the observed peer/resolver
records) is the counterpart axis and is out of scope here; its consolidation
is item-0004 (engine-cluster). The Engine observed-identity union stays as is.
