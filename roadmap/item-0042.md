Depends on item-0040 (the TLSEndpoint shape must exist). Golden-affecting:
the resolver record is sealed into the deploy attestation, so goldens
regenerate (same property item-0004 and item-0017 flag).

Coordinate with the dns-resolver arc: this item settles only the structural
endpoint shape of the resolver declaration. The behavioral hardening layers
on top of the settled shape -- item-0017 (combined IP plus hostname with
SAN/CN verification) and item-0018 (default port 853) -- and should be
authored against this shape, not the pre-merge one. The IP-literal-only
narrowing stays a Go boundary check with its intent recorded at the schema,
exactly as the current transport schema comment already documents.
