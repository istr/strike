Foundational for the lane-validation and DAG-reduction items. The step index is a lane lookup structure, not DAG state, and cannot be a field on the CUE-generated Lane; it is carried as a Parse companion. The iterators replace the stored resolved edge maps as the source of per-step views.

Amendment: the acceptance was re-narrowed after grounding. image-from was
originally listed among the lane resolvers, but its resolved image reference
is known only after the producer step runs (populated at runtime in
runState.imageFromRefs), so it cannot be a static lane resolver; it moves to a
runState-side resolver tracked as item-0069. The earlier OutputRef-return and
companion-resolver shapes were also dropped in favor of yielding the existing
lane reference types (InputRef, PackFile, name-keyed ArtifactRef) directly.

## Final summary

Landed in refactor(lane) commit 3240993: Parse returns the step index alongside the Lane and digest, IndexSteps carries the duplicate-step-name check, Build takes the index and holds it privately (DAG.Steps removed), and internal/lane/iterator.go adds the additive Inputs/PackFiles/DeployArtifacts iterators plus the step lookup helper. make check green.
