The CUE-side analogue of item-0093 (gotypelint). item-0093 folds the two
Go-type linters into golangci-lint analyzers; cuelint is the third Makefile
linter and stays outside that merge because it is a CUE-coverage gate, not a
Go-type gate. It is today a standalone on-demand tool, intentionally not in the
aggregate lint target. Keeping it go-native (analyzer plus standalone survey)
is what lets its Makefile wrapper fall away when the file is retired
(item-0096). Sequence after item-0093 so the shared analyzer/plugin idiom is
settled once, not invented twice.
