The remediation half of the BC-02 limit ruled in item-0072: the log/error formatting exclusion is scoped to the formatting expression, so safeName -- derived once from a primitive.Identifier and then threaded as a plain string parameter beside that identifier through executeDeploy, resolveImageDigest, checkCache, executePack and executeContainerStep -- is program state and D1. Sanitisation belongs in the logging seam; slog is the stdlib mechanism that puts it there.
