PARKED -- deferred, not blocked. IPv4 and DNS are the permissive-enough authority surface today and cover the ADR-051 registry push target, which may be an IP. IPv6 literals are out of scope until a consumer needs one: the change is not one line but widens the #Authority regex, teaches ParseAuthority and parsePort the bracket grammar (LastIndexByte(':') mis-splits a bracketless IPv6 colon), and settles how primitive.Host carries a bracketed address for the dialer. Surfaced from the ADR-051 push-target grammar.
