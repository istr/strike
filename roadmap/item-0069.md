Split out of item-0066. The image-from producer image reference is known only
after the producer step runs -- it is populated at runtime in
runState.imageFromRefs (cmd/strike/run.go) and consumed by resolveImageDigest.
A static lane resolver over the parsed lane and the step index therefore cannot
yield it, so image-from does not belong with the static inputs, pack-files, and
deploy-artifacts resolvers item-0066 adds. This item places the image-from
resolution on the run side as a lazy resolver: it derives the producer image
reference on demand from laneState (the artifact graph the producer already
wrote) when the consumer executes, rather than caching it in a runtime map. The
prebuilt-map-plus-mutex alternative was rejected because it would add a second
lock-bearing central structure, against the goal of lock-free parallel
execution. Removing the imageFromRefs side channel here is the first slice of a
broader run-state consolidation tracked as item-0070, which merges the remaining
per-step accumulators into one write-once structure and enables concurrent step
execution.
